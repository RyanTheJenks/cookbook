Sesame Pulled Pork Sandwiches
=============

Ingredients
-----------

### Pork
* 3 pork tenderloins, 1 lb. each
* 1¾ cups reduced-fat sesame ginger salad dressing, divided
* ¼ cup packed brown sugar

### Coleslaw
* 1 package (14 oz.) cole slaw mix
* 4 green onions, chopped
* ¼ minced fresh cilantro
* 2 Tbsp. reduced-fat sesame ginger salad dressing
* 2 tsp. sesame oil
* 1 tsp. ginger
* 1 tsp. reduced sodium soy sauce

Instructions
-------------

1. Place pork in a 5-qt. slow cooker; add ¾ cup salad dressing, turning to coat. Cook covered on low 4-5 hours or until meat is tender.
2. Remove pork; cool slightly. Shred and return to slow cooker. Stir in brown sugar and remaining salad dressing. Cook 30-45 minutes longer or until heated through.
3. Combine all coleslaw ingredients. Serve pork on buns with coleslaw and, if desired, wasabi mayonnaise.
